Research Interests


My research interests lie at the intersection of machine learning and control theory, with a particular focus on data-driven system identification and control for robotic applications. Ultimately, I aim to develop robotic agents that can actively learn their dynamics and remain resilient to sudden faults or malfunctions, even in contact-rich environments.
